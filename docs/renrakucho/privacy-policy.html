Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the れんらくちょう / CommNote (Renrakucho) app for mobile devices, together with any related services operated by Gen Takagaki (collectively, the "Application"). Gen Takagaki is hereby referred to as the "Service Provider".


Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include information such as

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address
  • The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages
  • The time spent on the Application
  • The mobile operating system you use
  • Your device's advertising identifier (such as the IDFA on iOS or Advertising ID on Android), used by advertising partners to serve and measure ads on the free tier

The Application also stores information you actively provide in order to deliver its core features, including the display name you enter, the group (shared notebook) you create or join, and the content you add to the shared notebook — such as messages and shopping items ("れんらく"), rules and agreements ("きめごと"), and expense records ("おかね", including amount, description, who paid, who it was for, date, and notes). This content is shared with the other members of the group you belong to so that you can use the notebook together.


Authentication and Account Linking

By default the Application signs you in anonymously; you only provide a display name. You may optionally link or sign in with your Apple ID or Google account so that the same data can be used across devices and restored after reinstalling. When you do so, the Service Provider receives an account identifier and, where you provide it, your email address. The Service Provider uses this only to identify your account and keep your data associated with you.


Push Notifications

If you allow notifications, the Application stores a push notification registration token (Firebase Cloud Messaging token) and your language preference so that it can send you notifications about activity in your group (for example, when a member adds an item, completes a task, adds a rule, or records an expense). You can turn off notifications at any time in your device settings.


Subscriptions and Purchases

The Application offers an optional auto-renewable subscription ("れんらくちょうプレミアム / CommNote Premium"). Purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play); the Service Provider does not receive or store your full payment card details. The Service Provider uses RevenueCat, Inc. to manage and verify subscription entitlements. Through this process the Service Provider receives purchase-related information such as the product purchased, purchase and expiration dates, transaction and subscription identifiers, the store and environment, renewal status, and a pseudonymous application user identifier (the identifier of the group the subscription unlocks). This information is used to enable Premium features for the members of the corresponding group and to provide customer support. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy (https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy), Apple's Privacy Policy, and Google's Privacy Policy.


Read-only Share Links

The Application lets you create a read-only web link to share a selected list with people who do not have the app. When you create such a link, the selected content becomes viewable by anyone who has the link until you revoke it or it expires. Only the content you choose to include is shared. You can revoke a share link at any time from within the Application.


Cookies and tracking technologies

The Application or its third-party SDKs may use cookies, SDKs, pixels, and similar technologies to support functionality, analytics, or service delivery. Where required by applicable law, the Service Provider will obtain consent before using non-essential tracking technologies.


Analytics

The Application uses Google Analytics for Firebase (Firebase Analytics) to understand how the Application is used so that the Service Provider can improve it. This service collects information such as a pseudonymous app-instance identifier, device and operating system information, and events about your interactions with the Application (for example, screen views and actions such as signing in, creating a group, or recording an expense). For more information about how Google handles data, see Google's Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy).


Advertising

On the free tier, the Application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob is an advertising service provided by Google. To serve and measure ads, AdMob and its partners may collect and process data such as your device's advertising identifier, IP address, and information about your interactions with ads. This data may be used to deliver personalized (interest-based) ads where permitted, and to measure ad performance and prevent fraud. Subscribing to Premium removes ads for all members of the group.

For more information about how Google collects and uses data through AdMob, please refer to Google's Privacy & Terms (https://policies.google.com/privacy) and Google's information on how it uses data from sites or apps that use its services (https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

You can opt out of personalized advertising. On iOS, you can limit ad tracking or decline the App Tracking Transparency prompt; on Android, you can opt out of ads personalization or reset your advertising identifier in your device settings. You can also manage Google ad personalization at https://adssettings.google.com.


Third-Party Services

The Application relies on the following third-party services. Your data may be processed and stored on their servers.

  • Google Firebase — Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Functions, and Firebase Cloud Messaging (to authenticate users, store and synchronize the content described above, and send notifications)
  • Google Analytics for Firebase (usage analytics)
  • Google AdMob (advertising, free tier only)
  • RevenueCat, Inc. (subscription management)
  • Apple (App Store billing and, if you choose, Sign in with Apple)
  • Google (Google Play billing and, if you choose, Google sign-in)

Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy) and RevenueCat's Privacy Policy (https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy) for information about how those providers handle data.
